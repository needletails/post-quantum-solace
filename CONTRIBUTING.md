Thank you for your interest in contributing!

This ensures that contributions can be used in both open-source and commercial versions of the software.

## Contributor Representations

You represent that:

- You have the legal right to submit the contribution;
- The contribution is your original work or you have the necessary rights to submit it.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch
3. Make your changes
4. Submit a pull request

## Review Process

All contributions are reviewed before acceptance. The maintainer reserves the right to accept or reject contributions at their discretion.
